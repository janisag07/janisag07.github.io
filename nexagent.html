Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do AI agents work? ▼

Our AI agents are powered by advanced language models trained on your business data. They understand context, learn from interactions, and handle tasks autonomously while maintaining your brand voice.

Is my data secure? ▼

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256), are GDPR compliant, and host all data in EU servers. Your data is never used to train models for other customers.

Can I integrate with my existing tools? ▼

Yes! We integrate with 100+ tools including Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Zendesk, and more. Custom integrations are available for Professional and Enterprise plans.

What's included in the free trial? ▼

Full access to your chosen plan for 14 days. No credit card required. We'll set up your agents and provide onboarding support to ensure you see value immediately.

How long does setup take? ▼

Most deployments go live within 24-48 hours. Enterprise customers with complex requirements may need 3-5 days for full integration and custom training.
