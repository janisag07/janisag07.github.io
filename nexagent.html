FAQ

Everything you need to know

What happens after I subscribe? ▼

After subscribing, you'll receive an email with next steps. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to configure your AI agents. Setup typically takes 24-48h, then your agents go live.

How long does setup take? ▼

Most setups are completed within 24-48 hours. For Enterprise customers with complex integrations, it may take up to 1 week. We'll keep you updated every step of the way.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes! No lock-in contracts. You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account dashboard. Your agents will continue working until the end of your billing period.

How secure is my data? ▼

We're GDPR compliant with EU data hosting. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We follow zero-knowledge architecture wherever possible. Your data stays yours, always.

What is Custom Training? ▼

Custom Training means we fine-tune your AI agents on your specific data (docs, FAQs, past conversations). This makes them smarter and more accurate for your business. Available in Professional and Business plans.

What integrations do you support? ▼

We support HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, Email, and many more. Custom integrations are available for Business and Enterprise plans. Just tell us what you need.

Can I upgrade my plan later? ▼

Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime from your dashboard. The price difference will be prorated, and your new features activate immediately.
