Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is an AI Agent? ▼

An AI agent is a software program powered by artificial intelligence that can perform tasks autonomously. Think of it as a digital employee that works 24/7, learns from your business, and handles repetitive tasks so you can focus on growth.

Do I need technical skills? ▼

Absolutely not! NexAgent is designed for business owners, not developers. Our one-click setup takes just minutes, and our support team is always ready to help if needed.

What's the setup time? ▼

Most agents are up and running in under 10 minutes. Complex integrations might take a bit longer, but we're talking minutes, not days or weeks.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes! No lock-in, no long-term contracts. Cancel your subscription anytime with just one click. We believe in earning your business every month.

How does 'Bring Your Own API Key' work? ▼

You use your own API keys from providers like OpenAI, Claude, or others. This means you pay AI costs directly to the provider at their rates, and we only charge for the platform and support. Total transparency, no markup.

Is my data safe? ▼

Absolutely. We're GDPR compliant, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we follow a zero-knowledge architecture wherever possible. Your data stays yours, always.
