Sales Sniper

Your AI-Powered LinkedIn Engagement Assistant. Never miss a high-value conversation again. Let AI find the signals, draft the perfect reply, and help you close more deals.

Powerful Features Built for Sales Pros

🔍

Signal Detection

Automatically scans your LinkedIn feed for high-intent keywords like "looking for", "hiring", "budget approved", and "need help with". Never miss an opportunity buried in your feed again.

🤖

Context AI

Analyzes the full context of each post, including the author's profile, company, and conversation thread. Generates personalized replies that sound natural and build genuine relationships.

📚

Knowledge Base

Train the AI on your product details, case studies, and common objections. Every suggestion is tailored to your unique value proposition and sales methodology.

💳

Credit System

Pay only for what you use. Each AI suggestion costs 1 credit. Free tier includes 50 credits/month. Pro plan offers unlimited suggestions with priority processing.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Track your engagement rate, reply success rate, and deals influenced by Sales Sniper. See which keywords drive the most conversations and optimize your strategy accordingly.

How Sales Sniper Works

1

Install Extension

Add Sales Sniper to Chrome in one click. No complex setup or configuration required. Works seamlessly with LinkedIn's interface.

2

Our AI continuously monitors your LinkedIn feed for high-intent signals. Keywords, contexts, and opportunities are automatically detected in real-time.

Engage & Close
