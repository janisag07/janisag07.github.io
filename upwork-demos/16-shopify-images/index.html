Pipeline Steps

1

Trigger: New Product Added

Workflow triggers automatically when a new product is created in Shopify, or can be run manually on existing products.

2

Background Removal

Use remove.bg API or rembg to isolate the product from its original background, creating a clean PNG with transparency.

3

Product Analysis (Claude Vision)

Claude analyzes the product image to understand: What is this product? What's the ideal setting? Target customer vibe?

4

Generate Lifestyle Scene

Based on Claude's analysis, generate a custom prompt for DALL-E or Midjourney to create an appropriate lifestyle background.

5

Composite & Upload

Combine the isolated product with the AI-generated scene, then upload back to Shopify as an additional product image.

🎨 Example AI Prompts

Claude Vision Analysis Prompt
Analyze this product image. Tell me: 1. What type of product is this? 2. Who is the target customer? 3. What lifestyle/aesthetic would appeal to them? 4. Describe an ideal scene where this product would look natural. 5. What lighting mood would work best? Keep response concise and specific.
DALL-E Scene Generation (Example: Coffee Mug)
Professional product photography background scene for a coffee mug. Cozy morning kitchen setting. Marble countertop. Soft warm morning sunlight streaming through window. Fresh croissants on plate nearby. Steam rising effect area. Shallow depth of field. Clean, aspirational, Instagram-worthy lifestyle aesthetic. --no mug, no cup, no coffee (we composite product in later)
