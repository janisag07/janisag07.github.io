📋 Executive Summary

This document outlines the architecture for an AI-powered sales intelligence system that integrates Snowflake data warehouse with Salesforce CRM, enhanced by AI agents for automated lead triage, scoring, and next-best-action recommendations.

The system will reduce manual lead qualification time by 60-70% while improving lead-to-opportunity conversion rates through intelligent prioritization and personalized engagement recommendations.

Data Flow

  • Snowflake → Historical customer data, transaction history, engagement metrics, firmographic data
  • ETL → Clean, transform, aggregate data into AI-ready feature sets
  • AI Agents → Score leads, triage priority, generate engagement recommendations
  • Salesforce → Update lead records, create tasks, trigger workflows

🤖 AI Agent: Lead Triage

The Lead Triage Agent automatically evaluates incoming leads and assigns priority scores, recommended actions, and optimal sales rep routing.

Agent Capabilities

  • Score leads 0-100 based on firmographic fit, engagement signals, and historical patterns
  • Categorize leads: Hot (immediate follow-up), Warm (nurture), Cold (auto-sequence)
  • Generate personalized first-touch message drafts
  • Route to appropriate sales rep based on territory, expertise, capacity

Example Prompts

Lead Scoring Prompt You are a B2B sales lead qualification expert. Analyze the following lead data and provide a qualification score and recommendation. LEAD DATA: - Company: {{company_name}} - Industry: {{industry}} - Employee Count: {{employee_count}} - Annual Revenue: {{revenue_estimate}} - Lead Source: {{source}} - Engagement: {{engagement_summary}} - Tech Stack: {{detected_technologies}} SCORING CRITERIA: - ICP Fit (0-40): How well does this match our ideal customer profile? - Intent Signals (0-30): What buying signals are present? - Timing (0-30): Is there urgency or a clear timeline? OUTPUT FORMAT: { "score": [0-100], "tier": "hot|warm|cold", "reasoning": "[2-3 sentence explanation]", "recommended_action": "[specific next step]", "personalization_hooks": ["[relevant talking points]"] }
First Touch Message Generation Generate a personalized first-touch email for a B2B SaaS prospect. CONTEXT: - Recipient: {{contact_name}}, {{title}} at {{company}} - Company Context: {{company_description}} - Pain Points (inferred): {{likely_pain_points}} - Our Solution: {{relevant_product_benefits}} - Trigger Event: {{trigger_event}} CONSTRAINTS: - Keep under 150 words - No generic "I hope this email finds you well" - Lead with value, not features - Include one specific, personalized observation - End with low-friction CTA (question, not meeting request) TONE: Professional but conversational, confident but not pushy

🛠️ Tech Stack

  • Data Warehouse: Snowflake (existing)
  • ETL/Orchestration: dbt + Apache Airflow
  • AI/ML: Claude API for reasoning, custom embeddings for similarity
  • Integration: Salesforce REST API, MuleSoft (if available)
  • Monitoring: DataDog, custom dashboards in Snowflake
