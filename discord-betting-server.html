🤖 Bot Features

📊 Affiliate Tracking System

Automated affiliate link tracking with real-time commission calculations. Members earn 10% recurring commission on referred premium subscriptions. Dashboard shows clicks, conversions, and earnings.

🏆 Dynamic Leaderboards

Real-time leaderboards tracking member engagement, pick accuracy, and affiliate earnings. Weekly, monthly, and all-time rankings with automatic role rewards for top performers.

🎁 Automated Giveaways

Schedule recurring giveaways with customizable entry requirements (role, activity, reactions). Automatic winner selection and notification system. Supports both free and premium-only giveaways.

📈 Invite Tracking & Rewards

Track member invites with automatic role rewards at milestones (10, 25, 50, 100+ invites). Anti-fake account detection and invite leaderboard with monthly prizes.

🔔 Pick Notification System

Automated notifications for new picks with risk level indicators. Premium members receive push notifications for high-confidence picks. Customizable notification preferences per channel.

📊 Performance Analytics

Track pick accuracy, ROI, and win rate per sport/league. Automated daily/weekly summary reports. Historical performance data with beautiful visualizations.

💬 Auto-Moderation

Spam detection, link filtering, profanity filter, and anti-raid protection. Automatic warning system with configurable punishments. Moderation logs and appeals system.

🎫 Ticket Support System

Private ticket channels for member support. Category routing (billing, technical, general). Automatic ticket logging and response time tracking. Priority queue for premium members.
